
# Customer Personality Analysis


## Introduction

The Customer Personality Analysis dataset contains customer demographic information, income details, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign responses. The dataset helps businesses understand customer preferences, spending habits, and campaign effectiveness. It consists of customer attributes such as age, education, marital status, income, product purchases, and campaign acceptance history.
## Objectives

The objective of this task is to clean and preprocess the Customer Personality Analysis dataset by identifying and handling missing values, removing duplicate records, standardizing data formats, and preparing the dataset for further analysis.
## Dataset Information

Dataset Name: Customer Personality Analysis

The dataset contains customer demographic information, income details, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign responses. The dataset is used to understand customer characteristics and improve marketing strategies.

## Customer Behavior Analysis

Customer behavior was analyzed using spending habits and purchasing activities.

Key Findings:

Customers with higher income generally spent more.

Wine and meat products generated the highest spending.

Customers with fewer children tended to spend more on products.

Frequent web purchasers showed higher engagement with marketing campaigns.

Middle-aged customers formed a significant portion of the customer base.
## Business Insights

Based on the analysis, the following business insights were identified:

High-income customers contribute significantly to total revenue.

Wine and meat products are the most popular categories.

Customers with fewer dependents tend to spend more.

Previous campaign responders are valuable targets for future campaigns.

Digital marketing strategies can focus on customers with high web purchasing activity.

Customer segmentation can improve campaign performance and marketing efficiency.
